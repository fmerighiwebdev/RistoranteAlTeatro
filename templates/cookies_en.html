Cookie policy

What are Cookies?

A "cookie" is a small text file that websites/applications send to your computer or other device connected to the visitor's Internet to uniquely identify the visitor's browser, to save information, settings and customizations in the browser.

Cookies for which consent is required

All cookies other than technical ones are installed or activated only following the consent expressed by the user the first time he visits the website. Consent can be expressed in a manner general, by interacting with the brief information banner on the landing page of the website, according to the methods indicated in it (by clicking on the ACCEPT button); or it can be provided in selectively, according to the methods indicated below. This consent is tracked on occasion of subsequent visits.
The user always has the option of revoking all or part of the consent already expressed (by clicking on the REJECT button) and you will need to refresh the page for the changes to take effect.
